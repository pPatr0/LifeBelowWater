Safeguarding Our Oceans, Sustaining Our Future

The oceans cover 70% of our planet, providing food, energy, and water essential to our existence. But pollution, overfishing, and other harmful practices are threatening these precious ecosystems. We must act now to protect and sustainably manage marine life, ensuring healthy oceans for generations to come.

Our goals for ocean conservation

REDUCE MARINE POLLUTION

By 2025, we aim to prevent and significantly reduce marine pollution from land-based activities, including plastic waste and chemical runoff.

Restore Marine Ecosystems

We strive to sustainably manage and restore marine and coastal ecosystems, strengthening their resilience and protecting biodiversity.

REDUCE Ocean Acidification

We're working to address the impacts of ocean acidification through scientific collaboration and global initiatives.

Promote Sustainable Fishing

By regulating fishing practices and ending illegal fishing, we aim to restore fish populations and ensure sustainable seafood for future generations.

Protect Marine Areas

Our goal is to conserve at least 10% of coastal and marine areas by 2025, following national and international guidelines.

End Harmful Subsidies

We advocate for the elimination of subsidies that contribute to overfishing, helping protect fish stocks and marine ecosystems.

Support Small-Scale Fishers

We work to provide small-scale fishers with access to marine resources and fair markets.

Uphold International Sea Law

We promote the enforcement of international sea laws to conserve marine resources and protect ocean health.

How can you help

Support Ocean Conservation Charities. Your donations, big or small, make a difference.

Say No to Plastic. Switch to reusable items and avoid single-use plastics like water bottles and bags.

Reduce Your Waste. Much of our waste ends up in the ocean. Make a conscious effort to reduce your waste footprint.

Organize or join community cleanups to protect local rivers, beaches, and oceans from pollution.

Buy Local, Sustainable Fish: Support local fisheries and sustainable seafood options.

Stay informed. Follow your local news and stay in touch with the Global Goals online or on social media at @TheGlobalGoals.
